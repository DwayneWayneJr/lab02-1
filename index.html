About Me

Ice cream scoopin, cocktail shakin, dog trainin, coffee addicted programmer.

Bio

My name is Ashlyn and I am 31 years old. I was born in Southern California and moved to Camano Island Washington just before my 13th birthday. I hated that I was forced to move here and planned to move back to California as soon as I turned 18, but quickly fell in love with Washington and never left.

Education History

In 2014 I joined the nursing program at Everett CC with a goal of becoming a Certified Nurse Midwife, (CNP degree) but quickly realized that playing with catheters was not for me. In 2016 a friend of mine told me that I would make a good programmer, so I dabbled with the idea for the last couple of years, and now here I am!

Overview of Job Experience

Most of my job experience is in the coffee industry. I started as a barista at 15 and ran a cafe by 16. In 2016 I was the accounts executive for a large coffee company in the Seattle area. While in that position I managed 4 cafes, and over 82 resturant and business accounts. I did coffee cuppings (fancy tastings) and got our coffee into Google, Facebook, Limeaide, Homestreet Bank and a few other companies.

Aside from the coffee industry, I also own a dog training business. My first experience dog training was as a volunteer for a K-9 training facility when I was 14.

I am also a bartender and specialize in craft cocktails. I work for a company called Revolution by the Barrel, bartending for private events and weddings.

Goals

My goal is to become a full stack developer and eventually work in the VR industry creating practical applications for everyday use.
